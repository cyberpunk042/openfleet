## 00_inbox

| Page | What It Covers |
|---|---|
| [[Verify Before Contributing to External Knowledge Systems]] | Verification-before-claim discipline. Contributing aspirational claims to external knowledge systems weakens the feedback loop. Validated through a specific 2026-04-16 self-failure: contributed a correction to the brain claiming OpenFleet lacked a root AGENTS.md — it existed. |
| [[Consumer Forwarders Must Route Contributions Past Their Own Wiki Root]] | When a consumer project forwards tool commands to a central hub, hub-bound commands (contribute, feedback) must route to the hub regardless of `--wiki-root`. Generic forwarders that auto-add the consumer's path silently misroute contributions. Evidence: OpenFleet 2026-04-17 misfiled 2 contributions before fix was identified. Brain already fixed upstream in commit `154bc58`. |
